Welcome

Rapha physio clinic is a long established physiotherapist institute
with over 20 years experience treating patients from
a range of background-elite and recreational sport persons,people suffering from
workplace stress the elderly and children with musculoskeletal problems.

Based in Eket Akwa Ibom Nigeria ,Rapha provides intuitive care for many musculoskeletal disorders including back and neck pain,arthritis complaints, bodyaches, frozen shoulder and plantar fasciitis. Our intention is simple: relieve your pain, restore your function, explore your movement and enhance your health and performance

Physiotheraphy

Our goal is to create a programme that is unique for every patient, we work with our patient to help heal injuries that stop them from living an active life this may include postural advice theraputic exercises, joint mobilisation, sooft tissue massage and electroteraphy.

Exercise Theraphy and Pilates

We provide a credible atmosphere for both treatment and gym,each patient recieves specifically designed exercise programme to promote and help return to full function.We also incorporate clinical pilates into many patients exercise programmes.

Orthotic Treatment

We help our patients to adjust the foot structure to achieve better optimal skeletal balance.A biomechanical assessment is carried out on each of our patients for better understanding of the cause of the problem,what treatment is needed and whether further investigation is required.
