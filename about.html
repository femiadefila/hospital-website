About the Founder

DR FEMI ADEFILA
FOUNDER

Dr Femi Adefila is a qualified physiotherapist with over 20 years of experience in his field . He graduated from the University of Lagos, Nigeria in 1989. He has an extensive range of clinical and administrative experience through his various spheres of work experiences. He started his career as Physiotherapist in Redemption Outreach Hospital. In 2001 he was employed by Mobil in Lagos State Nigeria where he served treating physiotheraphy and administrating care to all its workers and affiliates. During this time he was awarded the best and only physiotherapist the city of Eket Akwa Ibom State.
